  • All: Uncertainty in tail tends to follow reversal front.
  • OpenGGCM: Need to do at least one additional run with the BIMFz reversal much later 1.5 hrs (for OpenGGCM) b/c differences persist and vary with time for selected runs.
  • BATS-R-US: Most consistent after passage of reversal front.
  • SWMF: Biases exist at magnetopause and in inner magnetosphere after passage of reversal front.

Conclusions and Results - ρ percent differences

Intra-model comparison: No dipole tilt, BIMFz from +3.1 to -3.1 nT at t = 0.5 and 1.5 hr, BIMFx, BIMFy = 0, Vx= -440 km/s, n = 5.8 cm-3, T = 105 K. Percent differences clamped at +/- 100%.
  • All: Large differences before BzIMF reversal and decreasing differences after. Differences tend to follow reversal front.
  • OpenGGCM: Most consistent prior to reversal. Fluctuations in tail at ~ -10 and -50 RE.
  • BATS-R-US: Less consistent prior to reversal. Differences eventually go to zero.
  • SWMF: Least consistent prior to reversal. Small bias near magnetopause.
