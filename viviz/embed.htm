Conclusions - &rho;

  • All: Uncertainty in tail tends to follow reversal front.
  • OpenGGCM: Need to do at least one additional run with the Bz reversal much later 3.5 hrs (for OpenGGCM) b/c differences persist and vary with time for selected runs.
  • BATS-R-US: Most consistent after passage of reversal front.
  • SWMF: Biases exist at magnetopause and in inner magnetosphere after passage of reversal front.

Conclusions - Bz

  • All: Large differences before Bz reversal and decreasing differences after. Differences tend to follow reversal front.
  • OpenGGCM: Most consistent prior to reversal. Fluctuations in tail at -10 and -50 RE.
  • BATS-R-US: Most consistent prior to reversal.
  • SWMF: Least consistent prior to reversal.
